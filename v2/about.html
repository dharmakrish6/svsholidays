The Origin

A bad tour in Jaipur that started everything.

In 2012, our founders took a group tour through Rajasthan. The palace was magnificent. The itinerary was shared with forty-three strangers. The guide had given the same speech nine hundred times. The food was safe. The timing was rushed.

They came home and thought: India deserves better than this. Travellers deserve better than this. Six months later, SV Signature Holidays was a small desk in Chennai, two phones and a contact book built on years of personal travel through the subcontinent.

Fourteen years on, we design around sixty journeys a year. We've deliberately stayed small.

How We Work

The principles we build every trip around

01

Listen first, design second.

We don't present anything until we understand who you are, how you travel and what you're actually hoping this trip will be. The proposal comes after the conversation.

02

No property commissions influence our recommendations.

We advise on stays based on what's right for each traveller, not what pays us the most. Our income comes from a transparent design fee included in each journey's cost.

03

Fewer places, more time in each.

We push back on itineraries that try to cover too much. The best travel memory is almost never the third city you visited — it's the afternoon you spent longer than planned somewhere unexpected.

04

Our ground teams are partners, not contractors.

Every guide, driver and local expert we work with is someone we've met, travelled with and trust. Most have worked with us for over five years. They're part of the experience, not a logistics layer.

05

Responsible travel is non-negotiable.

We work with properties and operators who invest in local communities and environmental sustainability. We will not recommend a stay that we wouldn't be proud to explain in full to the people who live nearby.
